This is a medium hard sci-fi book with lots of great science thrown in from various disciplines. Yet there is always added the aspect of what it is to be human .. from the eyes of the protagonist who is not, wholly anyway, human. I enjoyed it a lot and it was quite the page turner. Recommended.

The Domes need to be opened, humans must be saved, and all must come to terms with the knowledge of how the harvest works. This phase of the mission may prove to be the toughest.

Spec is reunited with her remaining brothers and sister, so they can continue to move the mission forward. Living with their new neighbors, both human and others, will take its toll on all of them. Spec must successfully navigate an agreement with an unknown race of landscapers, in order to make the reset conducive for the remainder of humankind.

Every one of the expanding F&G team must work like the well-oiled machine that they were born and trained to be. Everything must fall into place, or no one will survive to see terra firma again.

*The Domes* follows Spec as she works to save humanity and move the Harvesters out of the inter-stellar neighborhood. Let the reset commence.

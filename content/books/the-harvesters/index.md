*The Harvesters* thrusts readers into the apocalyptic end of our world and the unrecorded historical void between the Earth's destruction and rebirth. Uncover the truths behind the rumors and conspiracies that surround us every day, as they may be true.

Led by Spec—a powerful, not-quite-human creation—the outcasts and brilliant misfits known as the Freaks and Geeks have assembled the sum total of human knowledge. Their mission is simple and brutal: protect what remains of humanity through the cataclysm… by any means necessary.

*The Harvesters* is the first book in the six-novel *A Spec in Time* series.
